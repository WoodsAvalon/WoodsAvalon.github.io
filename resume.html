Solar Car:

The senior design project for Electrical Engineering. As part of a team of six students, we were to research, design and ultimately build a functional solar car. Powered by low weight solar panels, power managed by a Maximum Power Point Tracker that adjust the resistance of the line to balance the voltage and current of the panels for optimal output. An 18650 Lithium Ion battery pack to store generated power with a 1 Kilowatt motor to drive the system. While many existing designs were available, referencing solar car designs completed by other Universities, due to available resources, this project required mostly research and design work with much of the fabrication farmed out to manufacturers.
